
# Ahoy Matey! Your User Guide to "Caverns of the Golden Isle"

## Welcome to Your Next Great Adventure!

Avast ye! So you think you're ready to tackle "Caverns of the Golden Isle", eh? Well, batten down the hatches, because you're in for a wild ride across treacherous beaches, through dense jungles, and into the very bowels of the earth itself!

## Starting Your Quest

1. **Setting Sail**: Open your command prompt, and navigate to the treacherous directory where ye have marooned the game files.
2. **Hoisting the Main Game**: Type `python game.py` and press Enter, as if you were commanding your parrot to take flight.

## Playing the Game

- When ye see `Ahoy! What be yer command? > `, it's time to show what you're made of! Type your commands like the captain of a ship barking orders.
- If you're feeling lost, type `help` and the spirits of the sea will guide you.

## Tips for Not Walking the Plank

- **Check Your Pockets**: Use `inventory` to peek into your magical, ever-expanding backpack.
- **Eyes on the Prize**: Type `look` to gaze upon your surroundings with your trusty pirate eyes.
- **Stay Light on Your Feet**: Don't hoard every shiny thing! Remember, your backpack has the weight capacity of a parrot, not a whale.
- **Make Your Choices Wisely**: The items you pick up might just save your neck, or get you out of a scurvy situation.

## Ending Your Voyage

- If the seas get too rough and you yearn for the dry land of reality, type `quit` to abandon ship and return to the mundane world.

## A Pirate's Reminder

Remember, the most important rule of "Caverns of the Golden Isle" is to have fun, or we'll make you walk the plank! (Just kidding, we won't. Or will we?)

So, what are ye waiting for? Get in there and find that treasure, you scallywag!

Yarrrrrr!
